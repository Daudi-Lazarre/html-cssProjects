A Man's Best Friend: The Dog

Updated by Daudi Lazarre on April 21, 2021.

Dogs are always there to comfort us, protect us, and do virtually anything we ask of them, and they teach us much more than we could ever teach them... In exchange for these gifts, all dogs ask for in return is that your provide for their basic needs, keep them safe, and give them a little understanding and lots of love. A game of fetch or tug-of-war is usually a hit, too!

How to choose a dog

Define why exactly you want a dog. Before browsing, consider your own health, budget, lifestyle and timing when choosing a dog. If and when everyone in your family is on board, the person spending the most time with the dog makes the decision. When choosing the right dog for you and your family, the primary things to consider are the energy level, age, size and breed. Incredible dogs can be found in shelters as well as private breeders. They can also be mixed or purebred. If you specifically desire the biggest version of the breed, males are usually bigger. Other than that, the sex of a dog should be a miniscule factor. Start your search at The Shelter Pet Project and Petfinder, regardless of the breed. Be sure to check the Reputable Breeder Guidelines. Most pet stores that sell dogs are from puppy mills. How to speak to a dog

Dogs live to commune with you ‚Äî because of this, any dog of any age can learn new tricks without you taking on a pack leader or dominant persona. Discipline is still necessary, and there are ways to communicate that in ways your dog will understand (i.e. without the aforementioned personas). Your dog is willing to learn how to communicate with you and what the boundaries of your lifestyle are. Your primary goal when your dog is first brought home is to bond with them. This is done with exercise, communication and consistency. By doing this, your dog will choose to obey out of love (inside out training), instead of fear (outside in training).
