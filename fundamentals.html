A Man's Best Friend: The Dog

Updated by Daudi Lazarre on April 18, 2021.

Dogs are always there to comfort us, protect us, and do virtually anything we ask of them, and they teach us much more than we could ever teach them... In exchange for these gifts, all dogs ask for in return is that your provide for their basic needs, keep them safe, and give them a little understanding and lots of love. A game of fetch or tug-of-war is usually a hit, too!
